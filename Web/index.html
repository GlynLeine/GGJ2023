Do you want to make a robot?

In Android Asesembly you get to take charge at the control panel of a robot assembly line. Watching the conveyor belt move and controlling the insides of the factory using the console, you get to play around with physics and get a better understanding of how the products and items you use every day get created.


The power is in your hands, you're at the forefront of the assembly process. This is your robot to make. You press a single button to start your assembly line. Once the parts have arrived you have to guide the robot through the factory yourself. Once you've completed assembly of the robot he emerges from the other side, down the conveyor belt into the shipping box. YOU have completed Android Assembly.

Want to assemble a robot yourself? Visit Android Assembly at the Maker Festival Twente.
